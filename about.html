About RUI TENG

RUI TENG OPTO TECHNOLOGY CO., LTD. is a Taiwan-based manufacturer specializing in dimmable LED modules, tunable white lighting systems, and Matter smart lighting controllers. The company develops lighting products for professional installation, architectural projects, and modern smart home integration.

Product Categories

  • D50 / D82 modular downlights
  • Track lighting systems, including 2-wire and 4-wire dimmable systems
  • Magnetic rail lighting systems
  • Ultra-thin recessed lighting
  • Outdoor wall lights and projectors
  • Matter smart lighting controllers

Certified Matter Manufacturer

RUI TENG develops Matter-enabled smart lighting products that support modern smart home ecosystems and cross-platform lighting control.

  • Matter Single Color Temperature LED Dimmer
  • Matter Tunable White LED Dimmer
